What it does

A personal style companion that helps you track outfits, discover trends, and get smarter outfit suggestions.

📸

OOTD Tracking

Upload your daily outfits to build a visual diary of your style. Track streaks and see how much of your wardrobe you actually wear.

✨

Personalized Suggestions

Get outfit recommendations based on your preferences, weather, occasions, and the pieces you own.

🔄

Recreate Inspirations

Find similar items in your closet to recreate looks from social media and fashion magazines.

📊

Monthly Style Recap

Review your style journey with recaps showing most-worn pieces, color preferences, and evolution over time.
